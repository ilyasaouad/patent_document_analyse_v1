COMMON IPC / CPC CLASSIFICATION CODES — QUICK REFERENCE
=========================================================

HOW TO USE
----------
These are starting-point codes. The LLM should select the most relevant
codes based on the actual claims and refine to the most specific subgroup
available. Always include both the IPC code and the closest CPC subgroup.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
NOTES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- CPC codes are more granular than IPC — prefer CPC subgroups in EPO searches.
- For hybrid analog-digital inventions, always check BOTH G06G and G06J.
- Time-multiplexing claims may sit in H04J (telecom) OR G06F 9/46 (computing)
  depending on the application context — search both.
- Hardware accelerator cards targeting scientific computing often appear under
  G06F 15/78 combined with G06G 7/48.
